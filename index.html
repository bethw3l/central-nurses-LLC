🎂 Birthday Greetings

Sends birthday emails (and SMS if Twilio configured) to active staff whose birthday matches today's date.

Uses template: TPL-BDAY (Email) and TPL-BDAY-SMS (SMS). Edit these in Templates.

Auto-schedule: Go to Apps Script → Triggers → checkAndSendBirthdays → Daily 8 AM

🎉 Holiday Greetings

Sends holiday greetings to all active staff on configured holiday dates. Manage holidays in the Holiday Calendar tab.

Each holiday can have its own email and SMS template assigned.

Auto-schedule: Apps Script → Triggers → checkAndSendHolidays → Daily 8 AM

📋 Credential Expiry Alerts

Scans all active staff credentials and sends expiry alerts for documents expiring within 7, 30, and 60 days. Also sends a summary report to admin.

Uses template: TPL-EXPIRY. Edit to customize the email format.

Auto-schedule: Apps Script → Triggers → checkAndSendExpiryReminders → Weekly Monday 9 AM

📱 SMS Setup (Twilio)

To enable SMS sending, add your Twilio credentials to Code.gs:

TWILIO_SID = 'ACxx...';
TWILIO_TOKEN = 'your_auth_token';
TWILIO_FROM = '+13165551234';

Sign up free at twilio.com → get a US number → copy your Account SID and Auth Token from the Console.
